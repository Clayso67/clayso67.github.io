Idaho Falls Ski CLub

The Idaho Falls Ski Club will be going on another ski trip this Friday January 19th. members of the ski club will receive 10% off gear rentals and lift tickets. The bus will leave at 9 a.m. from Idaho Falls Civic Auditorium. those who wish to get involved and sign up for a membership can contact Katie via email at katiesfakeemail@fmail.com

Come prepared with snacks and one clothes. Lunch will be provided. We usually get back to the Civic Auditorium around 6 pm.

If you have any other questions feel free to contact Katie and don't forget to invite a friend.

Boldering Leauge

The Climbing Gym is doing a bouldering League event. Kings of 4 can sign up to compete against each other and whoever completes the most bouldering routes wins. There's a $25 entry fee. All members of your team will receive a bouldering League t-shirt.

Bouldering League will be held on Tuesday nights from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. until February 31st!for more info visit the RexburgclimbingGym.org

Ammon Nights 2020

This Summer come join the fun at Michelle Clark in Ammon during “Ammon Nights”! There will be contests, prizes, food, and fun for all ages!

From March 1st to March 22nd 2020 admission is $7 bring a can of soup for $3 off admission. Hope to see you there and remember to bring a friend.
