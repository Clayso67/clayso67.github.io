My name is Kaylee Harris,

I've lived in Idaho for most of my life and one of my favorite things to do with friends is to go to the sand dunes and start a bonfire. It's always fun to listen to music and roast marshmallows while just having a fun laid-back time with my friends.

Hi! I'm Eileen Stephens,

If you're bored and looking for something to do in the area pretty close to Rexburg there is an awesome hike just outside Driggs to a gorgeous lake called Packsaddle. This is the perfect place to visit in the summertime while it's hot because the water is always nice and cool! It's a great place to have a picnic. You could rent paddle boards at the ORC to go out on the lake. Everyone that goes to Packsaddle loves to take a turn on the rope swing, so make sure to bring your suit!

My name is Jordan Hansen,

Last semester me and my roommates climbed Mount Borah! The tallest mountain in Idaho. It took a little bit of planning but you can find lots of resources about the hike online and even watch YouTube videos. We're able to do it in a day, and the view was awesome!
