I always find myself talking to those that are temporarily living in Idaho because of school. They find themselves far away from the places and activities they are used to. They haven't had the opportunity yet to find new hobbies and hangouts. It's easier to just stay in and say: “there is nothing to do in Idaho”. As a local to the area, I know of the potential goodtimes to be had, and my website: “ToDo Idaho” aims to be your local guide for the adventurous, and aloof alike.

On the local “highlights” page, you can find local people and businesses favorite things to do in the area. You might discover fun and simple date night activities, or long adventurous Journeys to go on.

The “bulletin” page will keep you up-to-date on current events happening in the area. It's the perfect place to look to see if there's anything fun going on this weekend.

And finally, listed below you will find my four personal favorite things to do in Idaho!
